.Modelica.Blocks.Math.Add

Information

This blocks computes output y as sum of the two input signals u1 and u2:

    y = k1*u1 + k2*u2;

Example:

     parameter:   k1= +2, k2= -3

  results in the following equations:

     y = 2 * u1 - 3 * u2
