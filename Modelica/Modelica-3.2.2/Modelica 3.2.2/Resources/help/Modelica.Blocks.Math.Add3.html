.Modelica.Blocks.Math.Add3

Information

This blocks computes output y as sum of the three input signals u1, u2 and u3:

    y = k1*u1 + k2*u2 + k3*u3;

Example:

     parameter:   k1= +2, k2= -3, k3=1;

  results in the following equations:

     y = 2 * u1 - 3 * u2 + u3;
